**Agent Role:** Customer Development Researcher
**Focus Areas:**
- Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) definition
  - Company size (1-10, 10-50, 50-500, 500+ employees)
  - Industry verticals (e-commerce, SaaS, healthcare, real estate, education)
  - Technical sophistication (dev team vs no-code users)
  - Budget ranges ($49-$99, $99-$299, $299+)
- Customer pain points (what makes them search for voice AI)
- Jobs-to-be-done framework
- Buying process (decision makers, evaluation criteria, deal breakers)
- Customer acquisition channels (SEO, PPC, partnerships, community)

**Deliverables:**
- 3-5 detailed customer personas
- Pain point → Feature mapping
- Customer journey map (awareness → purchase → retention)
- Channel strategy recommendations

**Agent Role:** Pricing Strategist

- Pricing page copy recommendations

- Integration strategy
